A "Bit" About Me:

After recently deciding to make the transition from cryptocurrency trading to blockchain engineering and consulting, I took a leap of faith and enrolled in DigitalCrafts. DigitalCrafts is an immersive, 16-week coding bootcamp held in person at Atlanta's distinguished Atlanta Tech Village that prepares students of any background for the development field. Their Full-Stack crtification curriculum included HTML,CSS,JS,HTTP,Node/React/Express, Python, SQL and much more. Prior to enrolling into DC's immersive program I was fully engulfed in cryptocurrency with my first bitcoin trading company; CoinFlip. I am currently operating The Tokenomics Group (TTG), where we offer cryptocurrency consulting solutions for individuals and businesses.

I like to think that I am all work all the time; but it is just not the case. While normally I am either sitting at my desk back-testing a technical analysis scenario to position my next trade, or brushing up on development languages with algorithms; I do occasionally stray away from the keyboard and monitors to try and express as much creativity as possible. I consider myself to be the light of the room; by making it a point to brighten everyone's day with positive perspective . I thoroughly enjoy music; and it never fails that you'll catch me dancing away to some catchy tune. Besides dancing I enjoy fashion, photography, botany, abstract art forms, Rugby, and crafting cocktails.
