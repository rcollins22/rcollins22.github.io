Project Detail

I was tasked with creating a online presence for Laurmani Naturals; a shop dedicated to providing it's customers with high quality, all natural skin and hair oils. This was an exciting opportunity to connect with a group and turn their concepts and sketches into fruition.

Prior to our introduction, Laurmani Naturals had no branding concept to build a presence with initially. I was thrilled to not only enlighten them about the untapped opportunities of an online presence; but to also help them achieve their marketing shift goals and also love their product.
